Convert a Menu to a Dropdown for Small Screens

When you’re on a small screen (iPhone shown here) and click the dropdown, you get an interface to select an option where each option is nice and big and easy to choose.

That sure makes it easier to pick a place to go than a tiny link. It’s two taps instead of one, but that’s arguable since you’d probably have to zoom in to tap the right link otherwise.
